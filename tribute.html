Unfinished Innings: The Rohit Sharma Saga

Journey from Unlucky Odds to Lucky Strikes.

CAPTIAN OF INDIAN CRICKET TEAM

Here's a time line of Rohit Sharma's Journey

  • April 30, 1987: Born in Bansod, Nagpur, Maharashtra, India.
  • June 23, 2007: Made his One Day International (ODI) debut against Ireland in Belfast. September 19, 2007: Made his Twenty20 International (T20I) debut against England in Durban.
  • November 6, 2009: Made his Test debut against the West Indies in Kolkata.
  • November 2, 2013: Scored his first double century in ODIs against Australia.
  • November 13, 2014: Set a new record for the highest individual score in ODIs by scoring 264 against Sri Lanka at Eden Gardens, Kolkata.
  • January 2016: Named the captain of the Indian T20I team for the series against Australia.
  • 13 December 2017: First cricketer who socred three double century in ODI international.
  • June 23, 2018: Became the first cricketer to score three T20I centuries during a match against England.
  • December 2019: Opened the batting for the first time in Test cricket during the series against South Africa.
  • January 2020: Named vice-captain of the Indian ODI team..
  • March 2022: Took over as India's full-time captain in all formats
    October-November: lead India in T20 world cup and went in semi-final.
  • October-November: Took over India ODI world and runner of the tournament (captained exceptionally),leading team from the front(Total runs-597)
  • 17 January 2024: Became the first cricketer to score five T20I centuries during a match against Afghanistan.
